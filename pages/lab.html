Common Baking Issues
Problem The Diagnosis The Fix
Cookies are flat as pancakes Butter was too soft or oven was too cold. Chill your dough for 30 mins before baking.
Gingerbread is rock hard Too much flour or not enough liquid. Adjust your flour and liquid ratios carefully.
Frosting is "soup" Too much liquid/milk, kitchen is too hot, or not enough powder sugar. Reduce the liquid and add powdered sugar 1 tbsp at a time until it stands up.
Cake is sunken Too much air in the batter or overmixing. Beat the batter gently and avoid overmixing.
